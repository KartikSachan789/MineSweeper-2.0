Game Instructions 📜

  • Objective 🏆: Reveal all safe cells without triggering a mine.
  • Reveal a Cell 🎮:
    • Computer: Left-click
    • Mobile: Tap
  • Flag a Cell 🚩:
    • Computer: Right-click
    • Mobile: Long press
  • Winning Condition 🎯: Reveal all safe cells.
  • Losing Condition 💥: Revealing a mine ends the game.
  • Tip 💡: Use the numbers as clues to avoid mines.
